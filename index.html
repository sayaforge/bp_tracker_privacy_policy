Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

BP Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the BP Tracker mobile application collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

BP Tracker stores the information you enter, including:

  • Blood Pressure readings
  • Heart Rate
  • Notes you add
  • Reminder settings
  • Application preferences

Your health data is stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or back it up.

Advertising

BP Tracker uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information such as device identifiers and advertising identifiers to provide personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

PDF Reports

The application can generate PDF reports using your stored blood pressure data. These reports are generated locally on your device and are only shared if you choose to do so.

Permissions

The app may request permissions required for:

  • Notifications (medication or BP reminders)
  • Storage access for saving PDF reports and backups

Permissions are used only for their intended functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Your information is not shared with third parties except where required for app functionality, such as Google AdMob.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no electronic storage method is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

BP Tracker is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
